Your Guarantee

We stand behind every fitting

Every windscreen replacement carried out by Autoglass comes with a lifetime workmanship guarantee. If anything with the fitting isn't right — water ingress, wind noise, trim problems — a return visit is free of charge.

✓  Covered by your guarantee

  • Water ingress traced to the screen seal or sealant
  • Wind noise or whistling from glass seating or trim
  • Adhesive bond failures (glass movement after cure)
  • Incorrect glass specification fitted
  • ADAS camera / sensor calibration not completed
  • Interior trim or A-pillar damage caused during fitting
  • Optical distortion or haze introduced during installation
  • Rear-view mirror bracket or wiring disturbed by technician

✕  Not covered by the guarantee

  • Stone chips or cracks occurring after the replacement
  • Water leaks from door seals, sunroofs, or body channels
  • Pre-existing corrosion in the screen aperture
  • Wiper blade wear or wiper arm damage
  • Damage caused by jet washing within 48 hours of fit
  • ADAS recalibration needed after a subsequent collision
  • Thermal cracking from pouring hot water on frozen glass
  • Tint variation within manufacturer tolerances
Common Post-Fit Issues

What are customers reporting?

These are the most common reasons customers get in touch after a windscreen replacement. Click any issue to book a return visit.

💧

Water Ingress / Damp Interior

Wet carpet, damp headlining, or water dripping from the screen edge after rain or a car wash — usually caused by an incomplete sealant bead during fitting.

Covered by guarantee
🌬️

Wind Noise / Whistling at Speed

A whistle or rushing sound above 50–60 mph that wasn't there before. Often linked to A-pillar trim not fully clipped back, or glass not fully seated in the dam.

Covered by guarantee
⚠️

ADAS Warning Light On

Lane keep assist, emergency braking, or forward collision warning lights illuminated since the replacement. Calibration may not have completed correctly.

Covered by guarantee
🔍

Optical Distortion or Haze

Blurring, waviness, or an internal smear that can't be cleaned. May indicate a manufacturing issue with the glass, or residue from the installation process.

Covered by guarantee
🪟

Wrong Tint or Glass Spec

The new screen looks noticeably different — a different tint, no heating elements, or missing the acoustic layer your vehicle originally had.

Covered by guarantee
🪛

Trim or Dashboard Damage

Cracked A-pillar trim, a broken sun visor clip, or adhesive residue on your dashboard noticed after the technician's visit.

Covered by guarantee
🔩

Adhesive Concerns

Visible black sealant appearing at the screen edge days after the fit, or the screen appearing to flex when closing the door firmly.

Inspect first
💥

New Stone Chip or Crack

Impact damage that occurred after your replacement — this is not covered by the workmanship guarantee, but may be covered by your motor insurance policy.

Not covered — check insurance

FAQs

Questions customers often ask

Can't find your answer here? Our team is available 7 days a week.

How long does the Autoglass workmanship guarantee last? +
Your workmanship guarantee lasts for the lifetime of your vehicle ownership and is transferable to a private buyer. It covers any defect arising from the fitting process itself. The adhesive bond is separately guaranteed for a minimum of 12 months, and ADAS calibration for 6 months or 6,000 miles (whichever is sooner).
I noticed water coming in two weeks after my replacement — is it still covered? +
Yes — the timeframe doesn't automatically disqualify a claim. Our technician will inspect the source of the leak. If it's traced to the screen seal or sealant, it's covered regardless of how long after the fit it appeared. If it's traced to door seals, sunroof drainage, or a pre-existing body issue, we'll explain the cause and signpost you to the right help.
There's a wind whistle above 60 mph that wasn't there before. What should I do? +
Book a return visit. Wind noise at motorway speeds is one of the most common post-fit issues we see, and it's almost always down to A-pillar trim not being fully re-clipped, or the glass not being fully seated in the dam. A technician will inspect and refit the trim or re-seat the glass at no charge. Please don't accept being told that "some noise is normal" — it isn't, and we'll fix it.
My lane assist warning light came on after the replacement. Is this covered? +
Yes — if your vehicle has ADAS features (lane keep assist, automatic emergency braking, adaptive cruise control), the camera or sensor mounted to or near your windscreen may need recalibration after replacement. If this wasn't completed or completed incorrectly, it's covered by your guarantee. Please treat this as urgent and contact us as soon as possible, and avoid relying on ADAS-dependent features until the system is confirmed as calibrated.
I jet-washed my car the day after my replacement. Could this be the cause of a leak? +
It's possible — the urethane adhesive needs at least 48 hours before exposure to a pressure washer or jet wash. Our technician will inspect and advise. If the jet wash has displaced partially-cured sealant, the situation can still be rectified, but the cause will be noted on your job record. In future, standard car washing is fine after 24 hours; jet washing should be avoided for 48 hours post-fit.
The new screen looks like a different shade of green/blue to my other windows. Is this normal? +
Minor tint variation between glass panels on the same vehicle is common and falls within manufacturer tolerances — this is a glass product characteristic, not a fitting issue. However, if your vehicle should have had acoustic glass, a specific solar or privacy tint, or a heated windscreen and the wrong specification was fitted, this is covered and we will replace the glass with the correct specification at no charge. Contact us with your vehicle registration and we'll verify the spec on your job record.
